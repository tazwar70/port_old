About

Hello,

My name is Md Shadman Tajwar Haque. I am from Bangladesh. I graduated in the year 2018 from American International University - Bangladesh (AIUB) with a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

I am currently an International Student working on my Masters of Engineering degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Windsor, ON. After completing my Bachelors of Science degree, I did an internship at Banglalink Digital Communications Limited, a leading telecommunications company in Bangladesh, as a Service Assurance and Experience Technology Intern.
